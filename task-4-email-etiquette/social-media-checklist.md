
# Responsible Social Media Use: College Student Checklist

## ✅ Do’s

- **Think before you post** – consider how your content may affect your reputation now and in the future.  
- **Protect your privacy** – adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing sensitive personal information.  
- **Be respectful and positive** – engage in constructive conversations and treat others kindly.  
- **Verify information before sharing** – avoid spreading misinformation or rumors.  
- **Balance your time online** – prioritize academics, sleep, and real-life interactions.  

## ❌ Don’ts

- **Don’t overshare personal details** – avoid posting your location, schedules, or private matters.  
- **Don’t engage in cyberbullying or harassment** – negative behavior can have serious consequences.  
- **Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content** – it can harm your academic and career opportunities.  
- **Don’t believe everything you see** – social media can present misleading or false information.  
- **Don’t let social media distract you excessively** – limit usage during study time or important activities.
