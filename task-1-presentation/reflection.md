## Digital Literacy Poster

I created this design using **Canva**, a popular and easy-to-use graphic design tool. The poster focuses on the concept of digital literacy for students and highlights its importance in today’s digital world.

My design explains what digital literacy means, including:
- The ability to use digital tools effectively  
- Evaluating online information  
- Communicating safely and responsibly  

It also includes sections on useful tools such as:
- Google Docs  
- Canva  
- GitHub  
- LinkedIn  

Additionally, I added tips for:
- Safe internet practices  
- Maintaining a professional online presence  
- Proper email etiquette  

The layout uses icons, soft colors, and well-structured sections to make the information visually appealing and easy to understand.

## Learning Experience

One thing I found interesting while creating this design was organizing a large amount of information in a simple and attractive way without making it look crowded.

A challenge I faced was balancing text and visuals to keep everything readable. Choosing the right icons and color combinations that match the theme of digital literacy was also difficult, but it helped improve my creativity and design skills.
