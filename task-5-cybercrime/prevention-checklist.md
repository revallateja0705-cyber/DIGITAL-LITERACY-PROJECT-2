# Stay Safe Online: Checklist for College Students (India)

## 🔐 Use strong, unique passwords
Create different passwords for each account (email, banking, social media) and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

## 🚫 Never share OTPs or passwords
No bank, app, or authority will ask for your OTP, PIN, or password—even if the caller claims urgency.

## 🔗 Verify links before clicking
Avoid clicking on unknown links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS. Check the URL carefully for fake domains or spelling errors.

## 📶 Be cautious on public Wi-Fi
Avoid logging into banking apps or making payments on public Wi-Fi networks in cafés, campuses, or stations.

## 👤 Limit personal information online
Don’t overshare your phone number, address, college ID, or daily routine on social media. Adjust privacy settings regularly.

## 💸 UPI Safety: Double-check payment requests
Always verify the receiver’s name before approving a UPI payment. Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN.

## ⚠️ UPI Safety: Avoid “collect request” scams
Fraudsters may send “request money” notifications claiming refunds or prizes. Decline unknown requests and confirm directly with the sender.

## 📱 Install apps only from trusted sources
Download apps only from official stores (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). Avoid modded or unofficial APK files.

## 🔄 Keep devices updated and secure
Regularly update your phone/laptop software and install antivirus protection to guard against malware.

## 🚨 Report suspicious activity immediately
If you face fraud or cybercrime, report it at:
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Helpline:** 1930
