## Project Report

For this task, I created and updated profiles on three platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each platform serves a different purpose in my academic and professional growth.

I used GitHub to showcase my projects, manage code, and build a strong portfolio. It helps me demonstrate my technical skills, especially in cyber security and programming. LinkedIn was used to build my professional identity, connect with industry professionals, and explore job and internship opportunities. Kaggle is useful for practicing data analysis and participating in competitions, which improves my problem-solving and analytical skills.

Over the next four years, I plan to actively use GitHub to upload projects, collaborate with others, and maintain a consistent coding profile. On LinkedIn, I will grow my network, share achievements, and stay updated with industry trends. Through Kaggle, I aim to participate in competitions and enhance my knowledge in data-related fields.

These platforms will help me build a strong online presence, improve my skills, and prepare for future career opportunities.
